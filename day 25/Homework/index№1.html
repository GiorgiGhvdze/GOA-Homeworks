Khinkali

Ingredients for 30 Khinkali: Dough – 1.4 kilo of flour (1.1 kilo for the dough and .
3 kilo for dusting and kneading) , 2 eggs, 450 ml of warm water; Filling – 700 grams of ground beef and pork mix, salt,
half tsp of dried red pepper, quarter tsp ground caraway seed, 2 small onions (optional) and 500 ml of water.
Cooking – water and salt for the cooking pot.
