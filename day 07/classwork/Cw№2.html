The Lamborghini Huracán (Spanish for "hurricane"; [uɾaˈkan])
is a sports car manufactured by Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini replacing the previous V10 offering,
the Gallardo.[5] The Huracá was revealed online in December 2013,[6] making its worldwide debut at the 2014 Geneva Auto Show[7] and was released in the market in the second quarter of 2014.
